# AI Literacy Plan — Power Automate Flow Reviewer Agent

Article 4 of the EU AI Act requires deployers to ensure users have sufficient AI literacy.

## 1. Who Requires Training

- Power Automate makers (flow developers)
- Governance reviewers
- Tenant administrators

---

## 2. What Users Must Understand

Users must understand:

- The agent is advisory only.
- It does not execute changes.
- It may abstain if documents do not contain the answer.
- It enforces scope and safety rules.
- They must not paste secrets or sensitive credentials.
- Recommendations must be tested in DEV first.

---

## 3. How Training Is Delivered

- 1-page Quick Start guide (included in repository)
- Example Q&A scenarios (correct vs refused behavior)
- Short onboarding session (10–15 minutes)
- In-app notice: "AI-generated. Verify before production use."

---

## 4. Evidence of Compliance

- Version-controlled training document
- User acknowledgment record
- Monthly KPI dashboard review
- Logged safety events for audit
