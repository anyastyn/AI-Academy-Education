## Risk Level Assessment

The Power Automate Flow Reviewer & Optimizer Agent is classified as:

**Limited Risk**

---

## Justification

The agent:
- Provides advisory guidance
- Does not make automated decisions affecting employment, healthcare treatment, credit, or legal rights
- Does not perform biometric identification
- Does not operate critical infrastructure

It is therefore not included in Annex III High-Risk categories.

---

## Applicable Obligations

As a Limited Risk AI system:

Required:
- Transparency (users know they interact with AI)

Voluntary controls implemented:
- Logging and audit trail
- Human oversight
- Data minimization
- Prompt injection defense
- Secret detection
- Quality monitoring via golden dataset
